Thor – The Thunder God

God of Thunder,Avenger, Wielder of Mjolnir.

Origin

Thor comes from Assgard and is the son of Odin. He’s got superhuman strength, can control lightning, and speaks like a medieval drama king .

Weapon

His hammer, Mjolnir, is legendary. Only the worthy can lift it (sorry Hulk). It flies, returns, and blasts lightning like it's nobody's business.

Why He Rocks

He's brave, powerful, hilarious (especially with his hammer jokes), and always fights for what's right — whether on Earth or across the Nine Realms.
